Djent (/dʒɛnt/) is a subgenre of progressive metal, named for an onomatopoeia for the distinctive high-gain, distorted, palm-muted, low-pitch guitar sound first employed by Meshuggah.

Stylistic origins: Progressive metal
Typical instruments: Electric guitar; bass; drums
